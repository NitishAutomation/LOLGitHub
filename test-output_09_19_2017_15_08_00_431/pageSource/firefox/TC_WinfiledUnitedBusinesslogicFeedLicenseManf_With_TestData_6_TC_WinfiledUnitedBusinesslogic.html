Feed License Manufacturing (FLM) Program / Quality Management System Consulting Services (QMSC) Program

FLM Program

The Feed License Manufacturing (FLM) program is an important extension of bringing Purina Animal Nutrition (Purina®) branded feed into certain market places through the manufacturing capabilities of licensed manufacturers. The FLM Program licenses an approved manufacturer to manufacture certain Purina branded products. A key aspect of this program is to establish and maintain the required feed safety and quality standards necessary for the licensed FLM manufacturers to efficiently produce and market Purina® branded feeds. Formulation and labeling support are provided to FLM manufacturers for these standard Purina® branded products.

QMSC Program

The QMSC Program offers all of the features and benefits of the FLM Program to dealers and manufacturers committed to developing and implementing a feed safety program with one major difference. QMSC participants are not licensed to manufacture Purina® branded feeds.

FLM and QMSC Program Offerings

Ingredient Specification Manual – This reference piece contains a listing of ingredients that provides general descriptions, nutrient analysis, physical characteristics, and potential aspects of each ingredient listed that could cause it to be rejected.

Quality Assurance Manual – This manual contains historic information on aspects of manufacturing feed. It addresses good manufacturing practices and standard operating procedures that may be adapted by manufacturing facilities.

FLM/QMSC Support – Available through training schools, Toolkits and direct communication on services.
Tools: Feed Quality Expectations Manual, New Audit and Self-Audit, Updated Medicated Feed Attachments, QMS Toolkit Resources
Training: QMS Training Series of three classes, HACCP training, Onsite training, Gap/Audit training
Services: Private and Custom Label Formulation and Labeling Services, Optimization of Formulas, HACCP certification/compliance program, Phone and Email Support and Regulatory Insights and Updates
